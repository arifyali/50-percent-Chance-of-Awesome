Network Analysis
This network consistes of two kinds of node, which we call "funding source node" and "candidate node". The edge from the "candidate node" to the "funding source node" indicates that the candidate receives donation from the funding source. This network only considers candidates' top 2 funding sources. And we summarize the funding sources as Consumer Discretionary, Consumer Staples, Energy, Financials, Health Care, Industrials, Information Technology, Materials, Not for profit, Not publicly traded, Telecommunication Services, Utilities.
